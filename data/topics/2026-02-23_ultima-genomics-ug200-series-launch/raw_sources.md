## Candidate Summary
Ultima Genomics launched the UG200 Series (UG200 and UG200 Ultra) and Solaris 2.0 workflows, offering up to 20 billion reads per wafer - doubling output. The new configurations provide higher throughput, better performance, and more flexible wafer configurations for population-scale genomics.

## Why It Matters
Advances the trend toward ultra-high-throughput, low-cost sequencing needed for population-scale genomics projects and large research consortia. Enables larger studies at lower per-sample cost.
