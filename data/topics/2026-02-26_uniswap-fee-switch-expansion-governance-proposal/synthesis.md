The move is projected to generate ~$27M in annualized revenue on top of the ~$34M already directed to UNI burns, sending UNI tokens up 15% on news.

3. **Strengthened Volume-Value Accrual**: With ~$61M combined annual revenue (burn + new fees) now tied directly to protocol activity, the UNI token gains a more direct link between trading volume and holder value—a model that could set a precedent for other DeFi protocols.

**Why this matters:** This vote represents one of the largest governance decisions in recent DeFi history, potentially cementing Uniswap as the first truly cross-chain, revenue-accruing DEX and setting a new standard for tokenomics design across the ecosystem.
