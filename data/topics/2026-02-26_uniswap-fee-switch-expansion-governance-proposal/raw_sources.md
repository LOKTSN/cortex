## Candidate Summary
Uniswap governance proposal would expand fee switch to 8 additional chains with tier-based v3 fee system, making protocol fees automatic for all new pools. Estimated to add ~$27M annualized revenue on top of ~$34M already used for UNI burns. UNI token jumped 15% on news.

## Why It Matters
Major tokenomics shift turning Uniswap into cross-chain revenue-generating protocol. Represents one of largest governance decisions for DeFi in recent memory. Vote would cement direct link between trading volume and UNI value accrual.
