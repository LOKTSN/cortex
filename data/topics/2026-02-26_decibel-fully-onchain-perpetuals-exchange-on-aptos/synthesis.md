# TL;DR

Decibel has launched its fully onchain perpetuals exchange on Aptos Mainnet, bringing a centralized limit order book (CLOB), deterministic risk engine, and native liquidity pool to the ecosystem after a successful testnet phase that attracted 700K+ accounts, 1M daily trades, and $58M in pre-deposits—with half coming from Solana and Ethereum chains.

---

## Key Insights

**1. Onchain CLOB with Sub-50ms Block Times**
Decibel's architecture places a full central limit order book directly on-chain, leveraging Aptos' sub-50ms block times to deliver performance comparable to centralized exchanges while maintaining blockchain transparency. This challenges the prevailing notion that onchain exchanges must sacrifice speed for decentralization.

**2. Deterministic Risk Engine Eliminates Oracle Dependency**
The exchange's risk engine operates deterministically on-chain, calculating position health, liquidation thresholds, and margin requirements in real-time without relying on external price oracles. This reduces attack surface and ensures transparent, predictable risk management for traders.

**3. Cross-Chain Liquidity Inflow**
Pre-deposit data revealed that 50% of the $58M came from Solana and Ethereum, signaling strong cross-chain demand for onchain perpetuals with better transparency than traditional venues. The x-chain account system and usDCBL stablecoin are designed to facilitate this multi-chain flow.

---

## Why This Matters

Decibel's launch demonstrates that high-frequency trading infrastructure can operate fully on-chain without sacrificing the speed or liquidity users expect from centralized venues, potentially accelerating the migration of volume from opaque traditional exchanges to transparent DeFi protocols.
