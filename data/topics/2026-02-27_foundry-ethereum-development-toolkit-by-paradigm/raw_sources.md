## Candidate Summary
Rust-based Ethereum dev toolkit from Paradigm. Forge (testing), Cast (chain interaction), Anvil (local node), Chisel (REPL). 10-100x faster than Hardhat. Solidity-native tests. 18k+ stars.

## Why It Matters
Industry standard for Solidity development. Performance and developer experience improvements so significant that most teams have migrated from Hardhat.
