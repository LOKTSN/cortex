## Candidate Summary
MindRank dosed first patient in Phase III trial of MDR-001, an AI-designed oral small-molecule GLP-1 receptor agonist for obesity and type 2 diabetes. The molecule was discovered using their proprietary Molecule Pro platform and progressed from project initiation to Phase III in approximately 4.5 years.

## Why It Matters
This marks a significant milestone for AI-driven drug development - one of the first AI-designed molecules to reach late-stage clinical trials. Success would validate AI platforms for end-to-end drug discovery and accelerate the field.
