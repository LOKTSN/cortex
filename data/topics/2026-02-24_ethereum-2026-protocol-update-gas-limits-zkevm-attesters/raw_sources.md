## Candidate Summary
Ethereum Foundation unveiled 2026 roadmap with three tracks: Scale (gas limits beyond 60M, ePBS, zkEVM attester clients), Improve UX (native account abstraction), and Harden L1 (quantum-resistant cryptography). The update aims to reduce L2 dependence and improve base layer scalability.

## Why It Matters
First major acknowledgment that L2-centric scaling strategy needs recalibration. zkEVM attesters enable parallel transaction verification without re-execution, potentially fundamentally changing L1-L2 economics and value accrual.
