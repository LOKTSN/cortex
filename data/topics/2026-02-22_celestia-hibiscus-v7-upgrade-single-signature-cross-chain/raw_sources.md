# Raw Sources — Celestia Hibiscus V7 Upgrade - Single-Signature Cross-Chain Transfers

## Celestia Prepares Major March Upgrade
- **URL**: https://www.bitget.com/news/detail/12560605212692
- **Type**: secondary


## Candidate Summary
Celestia's Hibiscus (V7) upgrade scheduled for mid-March 2026 introduces CIP-45 for single-signature cross-chain token transfers via Hyperlane, and CIP-46 for ZK proof-based message verification. Aims to solve sovereignty-interoperability tradeoff.

## Why It Matters
First DA layer upgrade enabling truly simple cross-chain UX - single-signature transfers eliminate multi-step bridging friction. Could accelerate institutional adoption of cross-chain DeFi by reducing technical complexity.
