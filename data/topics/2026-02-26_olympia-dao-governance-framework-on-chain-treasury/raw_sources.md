# Raw Sources — Olympia DAO Governance Framework - On-Chain Treasury Governance for Ethereum Classic

## ECIP 1113: Olympia DAO Governance Framework
- **URL**: https://ecips.ethereumclassic.org/ECIPs/ecip-1113
- **Type**: primary


## Candidate Summary
ECIP-1113 defines Olympia DAO governance for Ethereum Classic: modular Governor, Sybil-resistant one-address-one-vote, timelock executor, and ETC-domain restriction to prevent governance capture. Treasury is the sole authorized caller for withdrawals.

## Why It Matters
Production on-chain governance framework with explicit anti-capture mechanisms (identity aging, ETC-domain binding, non-transferable voting). Represents mature DAO design patterns beyond simple token voting.
