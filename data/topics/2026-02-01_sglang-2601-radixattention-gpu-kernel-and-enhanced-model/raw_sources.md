## Candidate Summary
SGLang release 26.01 introduces RadixAttention GPU kernel for efficient KV Cache management, plus support for new models including SANA diffusion model integration and openai/gpt-oss models. Nvidia collaboration roadmap shows Q1 focus on Nemotron V3 day-0 support.

## Why It Matters
RadixAttention kernel improves efficiency for complex generation scenarios with prefix caching. SGLang remains the preferred engine for structured reasoning and agent-based workflows, complementing vLLM's throughput focus.
