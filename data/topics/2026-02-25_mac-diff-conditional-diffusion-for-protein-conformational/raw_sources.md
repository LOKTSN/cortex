# Raw Sources — Mac-Diff: Conditional Diffusion for Protein Conformational Ensembles

## Conditional diffusion with locality-aware modal alignment for generating diverse protein conformational ensembles
- **URL**: https://www.nature.com/articles/s42256-026-01198-9
- **Type**: primary


## Candidate Summary
Published in Nature Machine Intelligence: Mac-Diff is a score-based diffusion model that generates diverse conformational ensembles for proteins using ESM-2 protein language model embeddings. Successfully recovers conformational distributions of fast-folding proteins and predicts alternative conformations for allosteric proteins.

## Why It Matters
Addresses a key limitation in structural biology - moving beyond single static structures to capture conformational dynamics critical for drug binding and protein function. Enables exploration of cryptics pockets and induced fit mechanisms computationally.
