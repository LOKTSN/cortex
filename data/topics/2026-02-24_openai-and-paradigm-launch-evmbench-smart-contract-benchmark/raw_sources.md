## OpenAI and Paradigm launch EVMbench smart contract benchmark
- **URL**: https://thepaypers.com/crypto-web3-and-cbdc/news/openai-and-paradigm-launch-evmbench-smart-contract-benchmark
- **Type**: secondary


## Candidate Summary
EVMbench is a benchmark designed to evaluate AI agents' capabilities in detecting, patching, and exploiting smart contract vulnerabilities. It draws on 120 curated vulnerabilities from 40 audits. GPT-5.3-Codex achieved 72.2% in exploit mode, compared with 31.9% for GPT-5.

## Why It Matters
Establishes first standardized benchmark for AI-assisted smart contract security auditing, a critical need as smart contracts secure $100B+ in assets. OpenAI also announced $10M in API credits for cyber defence work.
