## Daily briefing: New AI drug-discovery engine is 'on the scale of an AlphaFold4'
- **URL**: https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-026-00605-w
- **Type**: secondary


## Candidate Summary
Isomorphic Labs (DeepMind spin-off) unveiled IsoDDE, a unified computational drug-design system that more than doubles AlphaFold 3's accuracy on protein-ligand structure prediction benchmarks, surpasses physics-based methods (FEP+) for binding affinity prediction, and can identify novel binding pockets using only amino acid sequence input.

## Why It Matters
This represents a significant leap in AI-driven drug discovery, moving beyond structure prediction to actual drug design capability. The ability to predict binding affinities with gold-standard accuracy at scale could accelerate lead optimization and reduce experimental cycles.
