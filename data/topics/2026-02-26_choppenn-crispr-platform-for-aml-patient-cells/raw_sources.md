# Raw Sources — CHOP/Penn CRISPR Platform for AML Patient Cells

## First CRISPR-based platform to pinpoint drivers of AML
- **URL**: https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/childrens-hospital-of-philadelphia-and-penn-medicine-launch-first-crispr-based-platform-to-pinpoint-drivers-of-acute-myeloid-leukemia-in-patient-cells-302698641.html
- **Type**: primary


## Candidate Summary
Children's Hospital of Philadelphia and Penn Medicine launched the first CRISPR-based platform to identify genetic drivers of acute myeloid leukemia directly in patient cells. The platform achieved ~86% single-gene edit success in patient samples and ~73% success for high-throughput multi-gene screening.

## Why It Matters
This enables functional genomics directly in patient samples rather than cell lines, revealing patient-specific vulnerabilities and resistance mechanisms. Could accelerate precision medicine for AML and inform combination therapy development.
