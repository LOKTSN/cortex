## Candidate Summary
Illumina launched TruPath Genome, a whole genome sequencing tool delivering unparalleled accuracy even in 'dark regions' of the genome. The workflow generates 16 whole genomes per day (nearly double competing long-read methods) with only 10 minutes hands-on time, at $395 per genome.

## Why It Matters
This breakthrough addresses a major limitation in rare disease research - the ability to detect variants in previously inaccessible genomic regions. The simplified workflow and pricing could enable broader clinical adoption of comprehensive whole genome testing.
