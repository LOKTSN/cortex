# Raw Sources — T²-RAGBench: Text-and-Table RAG Benchmark

## T²-RAGBench paper on arXiv
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.12071
- **Type**: primary


## Candidate Summary
T²-RAGBench comprises 23,088 question-context-answer triples for evaluating RAG systems on text-and-table data. Challenges models to first retrieve correct context before numerical reasoning. Hybrid BM25 found most effective, but benchmark remains challenging for SOTA methods.

## Why It Matters
Addresses a gap in RAG evaluation for documents combining textual and tabular data, which is common in finance, business, and scientific domains. Accepted to EACL 2026.
